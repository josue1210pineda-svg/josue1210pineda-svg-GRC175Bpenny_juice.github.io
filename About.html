    • We're a family-owned business that was founded in 2001. We have over 40 years of experience in the beverage industry. Penny Juice was formulated specifically for children, and for use in childcare centres. Our juice concentrate comes in a wide variety of flavours that all mix easily with water and can be stored without refrigeration. We are proud to say that we have distribution centres in 48 states with free delivery across the country for your convenience.

    Penny Juice aims to provide safe and nutritious beverages for children while meeting USDA and CACFP standards, offering practical and cost-effective drink solutions for daycares, preschools, and educational programs. The brand focuses on promoting healthy hydration habits with products that are free from artificial coloring and low in sugar, while offering a wide variety of flavors to increase child acceptance. Additionally, Penny Juice seeks to strengthen its national presence through institutional partnerships, maintain competitive pricing through high-yield concentrated products, and build a trustworthy brand image among educators, parents, and caregivers.
